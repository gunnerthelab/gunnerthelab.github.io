# The Adventures of Gunner the Lab... Oh, and Tiger Too

## Illustration Prompts — All Stories

### Character Reference Guide (Use for ALL prompts)

**Gunner:** Large black Labrador Retriever, solid black coat, warm amber/brown eyes, hunter safety orange collar, broad chest, muscular build, floppy ears, big happy grin with tongue out when excited. Expressive eyebrows. Tail always wagging. About 90 pounds of enthusiasm.

**Tiger:** Tabby cat, gray and brown striped fur with classic tabby markings (dark stripes on lighter gray-brown base), golden/amber eyes, slightly stocky build, alert pointed ears, long whiskers. NOT orange. Expressions range from supreme indifference to quiet cunning. Carries himself like royalty.

**Dad:** Stocky build, beard/scruff, wears denim overalls (Dickies brand) and a wide-brimmed sun hat. Warm, expressive face.

**Mom:** Busy, capable, always in motion. Practical clothing. The calm center of chaos.

**The Three Boys:** Ages vary by era. Oldest is sturdy and serious. Middle is dreamy and imaginative. Youngest is small, wild-eyed, and perpetually in motion.

**Grandpa Bear:** Old brown mutt, grizzled, stocky, permanent grumpy expression. Gray around the muzzle.

Graphite pencil illustration, tight composition focused on the two dogs and the steps, profound stillness, exquisite detail on Bear's hesitant paw and Gunner's patient eyes.

## Notes for Image Generation

### Recommended AI Image Prompting Tips

When using these prompts in Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion:

1. **Start each prompt** with the art style: "Graphite pencil illustration, crosshatched, softly shaded" or "Colored pencil illustration, warm textured hand-drawn"
2. **Always specify** "black Labrador with orange collar" for Gunner
3. **Always specify** "gray and brown tabby cat, NOT orange" for Tiger
4. **Add quality tags** at the end: "storybook illustration style, children's book quality, detailed, professional"
5. **For graphite prompts** add: "black and white, no color, pencil on paper texture"
6. **For colored pencil prompts** add: "warm color palette, textured paper, hand-drawn feel"
7. **Aspect ratio:** 3:2 landscape for scene illustrations, 2:3 portrait for character portraits
8. **Consistency:** Upload reference photos of the real Gunner and Tiger to maintain character consistency across illustrations if the tool supports reference images
